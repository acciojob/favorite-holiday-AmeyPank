Japan

Japan is a fascinating country with a rich culture and history. It is home to stunning natural scenery, ancient temples and shrines, and vibrant cities. Japan is also a foodie's paradise, with a wide variety of delicious and unique dishes to try.

Top 5 attractions in Japan

  1. Mount Fuji: Japan's iconic mountain is a must-see for any visitor. You can hike to the summit, visit the many temples and shrines at its base, or simply admire its beauty from afar.
  2. Kiyomizu-dera Temple: This UNESCO World Heritage Site is one of Kyoto's most popular tourist attractions. The temple is known for its wooden stage that juts out from the hillside, offering stunning views of the city.
  3. Fushimi Inari-taisha Shrine: This shrine is famous for its thousands of red torii gates that wind up the mountainside. It is a popular spot for hiking and taking photos.
  4. Osaka Castle: This iconic castle is a symbol of Osaka and a popular tourist destination. Visitors can climb to the top of the castle for panoramic views of the city.
  5. Tokyo Skytree: This tower is the tallest structure in Japan and offers stunning views of Tokyo and the surrounding area. Visitors can also enjoy a variety of shops and restaurants at the base of the tower.

Must-try foods in Japan

  • Sushi: Sushi is one of Japan's most famous dishes and is made with vinegared rice and a variety of toppings, such as raw fish, vegetables, and eggs.
  • Ramen: Ramen is a noodle soup made with a variety of broths, toppings, and noodles. It is a popular dish for both breakfast and lunch.
  • Tempura: Tempura is a deep-fried dish made with seafood or vegetables that are coated in a batter and then fried. It is often served with rice and dipping sauce.
  • Soba: Soba is a type of noodle made from buckwheat flour. It is often served in a broth with vegetables and meat or fish.
  • Wagyu beef: Wagyu beef is a type of beef that is known for its tenderness and flavor. It is often served grilled or shabu-shabu style.
